OptiMeal

The Problem

The Team Novigi needs a website for their company Optimeal. The Wwebsite will allow users to plan and look for recipes based on their personal budget.

The Solution

Our Team worked together with the stakeholder to transform their rough prototype of OptiMeal into a complete responsive web design product. We accomplished this by taking user testing results and applything them to our design process.

Research

Millennials need a proficient way to plan and budget their grocery shopping because they find it complicated to calculate and abide by a budget. Because of their active and busy lifestyle, they need a way to find quick and easy recipes. Therefore, we are motivated to generate a daily tool for users that gives them a peace of mind and security in their daily life and in their pocket.

Key findings

We planned and conducted ten interviews to discover the painpoints that people encounter when grocery shopping.

We surveyed 73 participants, primarily between the ages of 21-40, who are considered frequent grocery shoppers that like to cook.

  • 93% of our surveys responders were millennials
  • Responders indicated that while they find these online tools useful, alot of companies that offer products like grocery delivery service are more of a hassle than it is worth.
